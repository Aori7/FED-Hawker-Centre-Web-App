About HawkerSG

Preserving culture. Powering convenience.

Who We Are

HawkerSG is a digital platform built to celebrate and modernise Singapore’s iconic hawker culture.

We connect food lovers, hawker centres, and stall owners through a seamless ordering and discovery experience, without losing the soul of what makes hawkers special.

Our Mission

To preserve Singapore’s hawker heritage while empowering stalls with modern tools that make ordering, discovery, and management easier for everyone.

Be Part of HawkerSG

Whether you're a foodie, a hawker, or a centre operator, HawkerSG is built for you.
