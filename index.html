About Us

HawkerSG is dedicated to preserving and promoting Singapore's rich hawker culture. Our platform connects food enthusiasts with local hawker centres, making it easier than ever to explore and enjoy the diverse culinary offerings that Singapore has to offer. Whether you're looking to order your favourite dishes or manage your hawker centre more efficiently, HawkerSG is here to help.
